About project

In Unity 3D create an environment such as the race circuit, country or city. Create a plot about two rivals competing to win the race. Create engaging action scenes with different camera angles and realistic physics. It does not supposed to be interactive. All animation sequences requires no user interaction.


Our inspiration for animation:


Our intention:

Make physicaly based animation of legendary race in GP Monza 1998 with Mika Häkkinen and Michael Schumacher, with some situation chages (weather...).

StoryBoard

Main story points and situations descriptions



Rainy Weather

Raining, wet track, low visibility from car (splash water from tires - fog effect)

Many Camera Angles

Traditional camera angles as top, behind, front... Special angles as top-cockpit view, front- view, view from curb...

Sounds

Every car has own engine sound (classic V12 engine), sounds of rain and watter, thunders...

Tools

Software which we are planning to use is going to be Unity Engine and Blender.

Story Points:

  • Schumacher is following Mika on final straight...
  • Before Variante GoodYear Mika softly overbeake front tires, Schumi is prepaering better atack position with ideal traction
  • Schumi is in Curva Grande closely behind Mika and is waiting for ideal overtaking position
  • Mika in defensive line before Vriante della Roggia, Schumi attacking from right (outside) side. Succesfull overtake
  • Mika is close behind Schumi in Curve di Lesmo, on lesmo straight is making fake attack on schumi and prepaering better position before Ascari
  • In Variante Ascari Schumi sucessfully defend his position
  • Mika is following Schumi on Straight before Parabolica, before Parabolica is taking inside position (right)
  • While driving out of the Curva Parabolica is Mika getting aquaplaning (drive out of the track, on sand ...)
  • Schumi drives through finish lane
